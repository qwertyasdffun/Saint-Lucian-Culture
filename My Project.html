The Project

The Theatre

As mentioned, the project/solution I created to sustain the St. Lucian culture and help the community is a theatre group.

This theatre group will perform plays which include the elements of St. Lucia's culture at the Cultural Center in Castries, St. Lucia. The public will buy tickets at a reasonable prices and be the audience to the performance so they learn and stay educated and involved in the culture. With the funds made from the performances we will continue to fund more performances, encourage youth memebers to join the group and help fund community expenses. As a result of Covid-19 protocols will be put into place and members of the group should be vaccinated.
