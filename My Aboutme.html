About This Website

The Creator of This Website

Hello world, my name is Jeanalla Samuel, I am 16 years old, I recently graduated from secondary school and I am the creator of this website. I am from St. Lucia, a beautiful Caribbean island with alot of culture and fun. However, as a result of Covid-19 I realize that our culture and liveliness is becoming less and less by the days and also is the funds which was provided by the various cultural events. So, this is why I decided to create this website and create this solution to try to sustain the culture I love so much and help raise funds for the community. Learn more about my project.

About The Website

This website was created to represent the colors of the St. Lucian flag(blue and yellow, black and white) to keep a consistent design and also make the website playful and fun to interact with.

My Mission

The mission of this website and this project is to educate individuals about St. Lucia's culture to help the culture remain strong, traditional and visible throughout the years to come. Additionally, I created this website and project to help raise funds by showcasing and performing St. Lucia's culture in a theatre to assisst the community with expenses and also fund more cultural activities.
